Travis Alexander

The man, the myth, the legend

Nov. 19th, 2020

My name is Lathe Ward, and I had the opportunity to talk to Travis Alexander about his experience at Base Camp Coding Academy, a nearly year long software development program.

How did you get into programming?

I was in 6th grade when I heard that over a hundred companies were hosting this event where you got paid to hack them. So I decided I wanted to be a hacker.

What has been the toughest week at BCCA?

The toughest week for me was definitely list week.

What has been the easiest week at BCCA?

the easiest week was week 2: strings, input, and print statements

What is your biggest victory so far at BCCA?

My biggest victory is my unit 1 project.
